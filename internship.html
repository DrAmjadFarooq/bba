Internship Requirements

We're looking for motivated individuals with a passion for financial markets and basic understanding of trading principles.

Essential Qualifications

  • Basic knowledge of trading principles and financial markets
  • Currently pursuing or recently completed a degree in Finance, Economics, Business, or related field
  • Strong analytical and quantitative skills
  • Proficiency with MS Office (Excel, PowerPoint, Word)
  • Excellent communication skills in English
  • Ability to work in a fast-paced environment
  • Willingness to learn and adapt to new challenges

Preferred Qualifications

  • Previous trading experience (even simulated/demo accounts)
  • Understanding of technical and fundamental analysis
  • Knowledge of risk management principles
  • Familiarity with trading platforms (MetaTrader, TradingView, etc.)
  • Programming skills (Python, R, or similar) for data analysis

Internship Responsibilities

As a Commodity & Forex Trading Intern, you will be involved in various aspects of trading operations and market analysis.

Market Analysis & Research

Assist in analyzing commodity and forex markets, tracking price movements, and identifying trading opportunities.

Trade Execution Support

Support senior traders in executing trades, monitoring positions, and managing risk exposure.

Data Analysis & Reporting

Collect and analyze market data, prepare reports on trading performance and market trends.

Strategy Development

Contribute to developing and backtesting trading strategies under mentor guidance.

Risk Management

Learn and apply risk management techniques to protect capital and optimize returns.
